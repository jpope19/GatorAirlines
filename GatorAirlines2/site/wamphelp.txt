How to set up your WAMP server for localhost

1. go to http://www.wampserver.com/en/
2. download the top left option
3. to start, click the shortcut
4. click the Green W logo on the lower right corner by the clock -> Put Online
5. blah/wamp/wwww becomes your working directory.
6. to access those pages, just go to localhost/pagename.php
